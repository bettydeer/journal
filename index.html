Which Learning Style Helped

a.k.a. sometimes experts are experts for a reason.

I chose "Skilled learners are process-oriented." from the list of 25 Things Skilled Learners Do Differently. I am always a future planner. This both helps and hurts me. I love the planning stages of things and then often loathe the process. I am trying to keep focused on the process and stay present. Yoga has been trying to teach me this for a long time, so the concept is not foreign. In this particular learning experience, I find I have little choice. It is moving fast and all I can do is stay present. It's like the hot yoga of learning!
